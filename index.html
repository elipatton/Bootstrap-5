Mama Baudo's Chowbella

Mama Baudo's Chow Bella is the newest restaurant from the Baudo's family. With a rich heritage of well over 50 years, the Italian recipes handed down through generations feature rich sauces, original pasta techniques, and home-made desserts. Delicious food! Located at 429 Walker Road Jackson, Tennessee. The restaurant features casual seating, on-line drive-through ordering, and eco-friendly beverages and packaging.
